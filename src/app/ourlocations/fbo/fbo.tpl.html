CUERNAVACA

Known as the “Eternal Spring City”, Cuernavaca is one the preferred destinations due to its closeness to Mexico City, its quiet and nice warm weather. The landscape is full of color and scents from all the flowers that are grown there as tulips, orchards and daisies; beautiful plazas and colonial style buildings shape the downtown of the city, crowned by the Cathedral. Due to the great amount of mountains, volcanoes, meadows and rivers around Cuernavaca, natural adventures are the preferred activities for visitors.
