  • ICCS is able to represent and assist legally to your company at General Direction of Civil Aviation “DGAC” in order to process Multiple Entry Authorizations for private flights, authorizing to an aircraft to fly into Mexican territory according to the government regulations as many times is needed.

    ICCS is proud to have an in site office of the DGAC’s headquarters, with specialized and dedicated personnel willing to provide the best service and accurate information.

    Part 91 aircraft are able to obtain:

    1. a) Single Entry Permit.- This permit can be processed if you fly just once a year through our 24 / 7 Flight Coordination Center. If you need assistance please contact: iccsops@iccs.com.mx
    2. b) Multiple Entry Permit.- This permit is suggested if you fly more than 2 times a year, it is valid until December 31st. of every year and it allows to fly into Mexico as many times you need. If you need assistance please contact: iccspermits@iccs.com.mx

  • ICCS is able to represent and assist legally to your company at General Direction of Civil Aviation “DGAC” in order to process Indefinite Blanket Permits for Passengers, Cargo & Ambulance flights, as well as all mandatory requirements involved to be authorized to fly into Mexican territory according to the government regulations. ICCS is proud to have an in site office of the DGAC’s headquarters, with specialized and dedicated personnel willing to provide the best service and accurate information.

    FAR Part 135

    Indefinite Blanket Permit is authorized for aircraft or fleet, smaller than 3.5 tons of payload or less than 14 seats.

    FAR Part 121

    Indefinite Blanket Permit is authorized for aircraft or fleet, larger than 3.5 tons of payload or more than 14 seats.
