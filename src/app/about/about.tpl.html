About Us

First Class Ground Handling Services in Mexico and Latin America
Corporate / Cargo / Ambulance

With more than 20 years of experience and over 300 highly trained and committed professionals, ICCS offers an umbrella of personalized Ground Handling Services to Executive and Cargo Aircraft.

ICCS handles around 20,000 flight operations per year for Customers arriving from all over the world.

ICCS owns and operates the number 1 Network FBOs and offices in Mexico, and provides Integrated Handling Support Services at all 73 Airports in the country and also at the major airports in the Caribbean, Central and South America.

Exclusive FBO facilities, the Best Jet fuel price at all airports, Ground Handling, Landing & Overflight Permits, Gourmet Catering, VIP Concierge Assistance and Security are just some of ICCS’ services.

Safety and Quality for Our Customers are our Number one Priority

ICCS knows how important reliable and timely service means for your flights; and our Flight Coordination Center is committed and eager to provide 24/7 Handling Support for Executive, Ambulance and Cargo Flights.

ICCS offers solutions and complete satisfaction to all your needs when flying to Mexico and Latin America, and assure Safety, Reliability, Efficiency and Quality in all of the Flights of their discerning Customers.
